About Me

I am a passionate theater artist and drama minister dedicated to storytelling through stage performances and gospel drama. br About Me Omowale Okelola – Actor | Director | Playwright | Drama Evangelist I am Omowale Okelola, a passionate actor, director, and playwright with a deep commitment to using drama as a powerful tool for storytelling, evangelism, and social impact. With extensive training from prestigious institutions like Mount Zion Institute of Christian Drama (Nigeria), EbonyLife Creative Academy, and Praxy Digital Academy, I have honed my craft in acting, directing, and scriptwriting, bringing compelling narratives to life on stage and screen. My Work in Drama & Theater My journey in drama spans acting, directing, and mentoring, with a focus on productions that entertain, educate, and inspire. Some of my notable works include: "The Gods Are Not to Blame" by Ola Rotimi – Featured as a lead actor in this classic Nigerian play. "Trials of Brother Jero" by Wole Soyinka – Acted and directed this satirical masterpiece. "Wedding Garment" – A gospel stage play, co-produced with my wife, which we take from church to church, delivering a message of hope and divine reward. Drama as a Tool for Impact Beyond entertainment, I am dedicated to mentoring and inspiring young minds through drama. I regularly visit secondary schools, using stage performances to encourage and educate students. In addition, I train interested crew members in drama during my volunteer missions, equipping them with the skills to use storytelling effectively. Volunteer & Humanitarian Work My passion extends beyond the stage—I am a committed volunteer, having served multiple times with Mercy Ships in Cameroon, Senegal, and other African countries. While on these missions, I not only contributed to humanitarian efforts but also trained interested crew members in drama, helping them harness the power of storytelling for positive change. Drama Ministry & Evangelism As the head of my church’s drama ministry, I am actively involved in producing and directing gospel plays, skits, and short films that convey deep spiritual messages. Alongside my wife, I am on a special mission to use drama as a means of evangelism and hope, traveling across communities and churches to stage performances that touch lives and transform hearts. Let’s Connect I am open to collaborations in film, theater, faith-based productions, and training programs.
